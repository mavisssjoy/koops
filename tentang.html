Palembang from the edge.

KOOPS is an independent media platform dedicated to curating the cultural landscape, urban lifestyle, and contemporary subcultures of Palembang.

We operate at the intersection of local tradition and modern youth culture, documenting everything from grassroots music scenes and the dynamics of new-wave hangout spots, to the untold narratives shaping the city's daily life.

Through intimate storytelling and alternative perspectives, we invite you to experience Palembang from the angles that rarely make the mainstream headlines.
